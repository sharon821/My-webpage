HTML Subscript and Superscript

Subscript

  • Subscript are usually rendered with a lowered baseline and smaller text.
  • The subcript element should only be used for typography reasons not for appearance purposes

example 1

N2+H2→ NH3

N2+H2→ 2NH3

Superscript

  • Superscripts are usually rendered with a raised baseline and smaller text.
  • The supercript element should only be used for typography reasons not for appearance purposes

example 1

(x+2)(x2+3)

=x3+3x+2x2+6

=x3+2x2+3x+6
